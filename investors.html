The thesis

Why the agent is the keystone

The licensed local agent is the single relationship that unlocks everything else — and it's the same person a serious buyer ultimately needs.

One relationship, three unlocks

A partner agent's license authorizes the real MLS feed (data + photos), funnels their sphere into the app (distribution), and lends local credibility the AI guide rides on (trust). Most proptech disintermediates agents; we make them the keystone.

Capital-light expansion

A market = one MLS (~$199/mo) + one partner agent. A single closed referral can fund that market for a year-plus.

Warm, not auctioned

We hand buyers to one trusted agent with their revealed preferences — a trust position lead-auction portals structurally can't take.

Built & shipping

iOS app on TestFlight, real-data architecture, AI guide, virtual flyby. Site live. The agent portal is the next build.
